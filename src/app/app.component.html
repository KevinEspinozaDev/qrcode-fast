FAQ

  • How do you create a QR Code?
    • This QR Code generator uses the library angularx-qrcode to create QR codes, so it because it uses a local installed library, it works very fast (as fast as a Rocket!)
  • What is a QR Code?
    • A QR code (an initialism for quick response code) is a code consisting of an array of black and white squares, typically used for storing URLs or other information for reading by the camera on a smartphone.
  • What is a QR code used for?
    • QR codes are most often used to store web links; however, they can store arbitrary data, such as text or images. When you scan a QR code, the QR reader in your phone's camera deciphers the code, and the resulting information triggers an action on your phone.
